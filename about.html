About Bibek Books

Bibek Books is a premium digital library curated by Bibek Patra — an entrepreneur, full-stack developer, author, and technology innovator based in Mayurbhanj, Odisha, India.

As the founder of BibekBook, Gengram, Vivika AI, and Krayvo, Bibek builds digital solutions that bridge the gap between creativity and cutting-edge technology. His work spans publishing, artificial intelligence, social networking, e-commerce, web development, digital marketing, and 3D design.

Our Mission

To make knowledge accessible to everyone. We curate a diverse collection of books across Spiritual, Fiction, Non-Fiction, Science, History, Poetry, and Business — offering both free and paid titles to suit every reader.
